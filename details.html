Effective Date: January 1, 2016Citation updated: November 18, 2018

Overview

Training required for direct care staff of residential care facilities for the elderly.

Governing Body: California State Legislature

[ Source ]

Training Requirements

Training Frequency: Training is required. As needed.

Content Hours: Four hours prior to providing care. After the initial training, a four-hour in-service refresher course each year.

Record Keeping: Training records must be kept.

Record Retention: Records must be kept for three years.

Trainer Requirements: Trainer must hold additional training licensure.

License Requirements

Qualifications: Training is required. As needed.

Reciprocity and Compact Agreements: Four hours prior to providing care. After the initial training, a four-hour in-service refresher course each year.

License Maintenance: Training records must be kept.

Ethics Rules: Records must be kept for three years.

Scope of Practice Requirements

Services/Treatments Allowed: Training is required. As needed.

Prescriptive Authority: Four hours prior to providing care. After the initial training, a four-hour in-service refresher course each year.

When does this apply?

Required?: No

For Initial Hires: No

Before Duties: Yes

Periodically: Yes

After Review: No

After Incident: Yes

Notes: No applicability notes are specified.
